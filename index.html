About

Hi. I’m Gary. From developing black-and-white film with my grandad to shooting in a commercial studio, photography has always been my world. I started as a retoucher in 2000 and soon moved behind the camera—since then I’ve worked across fashion, weddings, portraits, corporate and commercial, with work appearing on magazine covers.

Photos are for remembering and smiling over. My style isn’t rigid—I want you to relax, have fun, and see your personality shine through. If you like what you see and feel my approach fits, I’d love to hear from you.
